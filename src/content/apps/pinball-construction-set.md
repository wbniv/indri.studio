A cross-platform construction set for pinball: drag in flippers, slingshots, bumpers, and ramps; the physics simulator runs live as you build. Save your tables locally, share them, play them.

Built in [Flutter](https://flutter.dev/). Mobile via the [App Store](https://www.apple.com/app-store/) and [Google Play](https://play.google.com/); desktop (Linux, Mac, Windows) and **[Steam Deck](https://www.steamdeck.com/)** via [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/). Deterministic physics — fast, intuitive, fun, not realistic.
